Storm Events Database¶

The purpose of this Jupyter Notebook is to demonstrate how to read the .csv files from the SPC Storm Events Database.

Where did the data come from?¶

The data were downloaded from the NOAA National Center for Environmental Information.
Details about the database and the data files are available here: https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/stormevents/details.jsp

Where is the data located on SoM Computers?¶

The data are located in: /data/esplab/shared/classes/metr3334/class_data/stormevents/

What Files are there?¶

  • StormEvents_details*.csv
  • StormEvents_fatalities*.csv
  • StormEvents_locations*.csv
  • stormdata_*.csv

How do I understand what is in each file?¶

  • Storm-Data-Bulk-csv-Format.pdf
  • Storm-Data-Export-Format.pdf
